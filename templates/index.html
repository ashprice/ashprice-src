Bibliographies

These are some annotated bibliographies on topics that interest me. They are posted mostly for personal reference, but some might find utility in them as well.

I will cite whole works unless there is some reason to emphasise specific chapters of handbooks and conference proceedings. In a few cases, I will make reference to all the works of a single author. Order will be alphabetical or in one I deem helpful.

The symbols rap at the end of entries are a personal notation. Don't worry about them.. $bibs$
