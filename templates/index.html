Bibliographies

These are some annotated bibliographies on topics that interest me. They are posted mostly for personal reference, but someone might find some utility in them as well.

In general, I will cite whole works unless there is some reason to emphasise specific chapters of handbooks and conference proceedings (depending on the context, I may do so with sub-items or by entering different chapters under different headings). In a few cases, I will make reference to all the works of a single author. Order will variously be alphabetical or in the order I think it makes sense to give them.
